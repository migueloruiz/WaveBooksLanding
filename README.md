# Wave Books

**Your personal audiobook library, offline.**

Wave Books is an offline-first audiobook player for iPhone. Bring your own
DRM-free audiobooks, keep them on your device, and listen anywhere — no account
required, no subscription, no ads.

🌐 **[wavebooks.app](https://wavebooks.app)**

## What you can do

- **Import your books** — from local files, iCloud Drive, AudiobookShelf, or your
  own supported server.
- **Organize your library** — keep your stories tidy and easy to browse.
- **Listen offline** — everything lives on your iPhone, so you can play anywhere,
  with no signal needed.
- **Playback your way** — personalized controls tuned for how you like to listen.

## Where your books come from

- **Local Files** — audiobooks already on your device.
- **iCloud Drive** — sync your collection across Apple devices.
- **AudiobookShelf** — connect to your own AudiobookShelf server.
- **Jellyfin** — bring in books from your Jellyfin library.

## Privacy first

Your library stays yours. Wave Books is built to work offline, so your books and
your listening habits stay on your device.
